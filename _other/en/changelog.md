### v0.4.0

* Features for team
<br> **a)** Support Project templates. ([Details][4])
<br> **b)** [AutoImport][3]
* Added options for [Text Injection][1]:
<br> **a)** Start of File, End of line
<br> **b)** End of File, Start of line

* fixed little bugs.
